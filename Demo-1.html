Frequently Asked Questions

If you have a question that's not answered below, please get in touch.
When will my website go live?

From kick-off to launch, the timeline is usually 4 weeks.

Do I need to write the content myself?

Just send over your core details - services, fees, team info, and anything else you'd like included. I’ll write everything up and send it to you for review before we go live.

Do I need to get a professional photographer?

I always recommend showing staff faces as it increases trust and familiarity for website visitors.

Getting a professional photographer is optional - it can be a good idea to get nice photos of your staff, team and practice. However, it is not always necessary to get a professional photographer - sometimes a good phone camera in the right lighting can work fine for simple headshots.

Stock photos and icons will be used in other areas as required.

Is branding required before we start?

If you have one, great. If not, I can design a simple, professional logo for you and build the rest of the website to match.

How do customers pay through the website?

Payments are handled securely via Stripe. It’s quick to set up, and funds are transferred to your bank account automatically. Stripe charges 1.5% + 25c per transaction.

Can I make changes to the website myself?

Yes - you’ll have access to an easy-to-use CMS to update text and images. Design or layout changes require my input and are quoted separately.

Can I get a grant to help cover the cost?

Yes, the Local Enterprise Office offers the Grow Digital Voucher, which covers up to 50% of eligible costs, up to €5,000. It’s designed to help small businesses invest in new digital tools - such as websites, booking systems, CRMs, or other online platforms to improve how you operate and serve patients.

If your project costs €4,000, for example, you could receive €2,000 back through the voucher.

Will I need to pay for updates or maintenance?

No monthly maintenance is needed - Webflow sites are fully hosted and self-updating. You’ll only need to cover annual hosting, and larger updates are priced on request.

Can you help set up email addresses for my clinic?

Yes - I can set up Google Workspace accounts like info@yourclinic.ie. Setup is €150, with an additional €100 for email migration. Google charges €80/year per user.

Will my website be GDPR compliant?

Yes - all forms are fully GDPR compliant. Personal data is handled securely and can be deleted upon request, keeping you in line with current regulations.

Who hosts and manages the website?

I take care of hosting and management through Webflow - a modern, reliable platform with fast load times, 99.99% uptime, and built-in SSL for security.

How secure is the website and data?

The site is hosted on Webflow, which offers secure infrastructure, continuous monitoring, and compliance with GDPR and SOC 2 standards. Access is secured with 2-Factor Authentication.
